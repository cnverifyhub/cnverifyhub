## Skrill: The Agile Hub for Modern Payments

Skrill (formerly Moneybookers) has won over millions with its streamlined user experience and top-tier mobile app. It feels more like a modern neo-bank than a traditional e-wallet.

### Why Choose Skrill?

*   **Crypto-Forward**: Built-in crypto buying and selling for over 40 digital assets with a very intuitive UI.
*   **Skrill Knect**: Earn points for every dollar spent or transferred, which can be redeemed for cash—a rare feature in the e-wallet space.
