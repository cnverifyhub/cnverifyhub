## Payoneer: The Bedrock for Platform Sellers

Founded in 2005, Payoneer is the official payout partner for Amazon, Shopee, and Airbnb. Its core strength lies in its ability to handle complex, high-volume corporate transactions.

### Why Choose Payoneer?
*   **Deep Ecosystem Integration**: Most major marketplaces have Payoneer built into their dashboard for one-click payouts.
*   **Local Bank Details**: Get bank account numbers in 10+ currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, etc.) to receive funds like a local business.
*   **Robust B2B Features**: Request payments directly from global clients and accept credit card payments with ease.

## Wise: The Modern 'Exchange Rate Master'

Wise was built on the premise that transferring money should be as cheap and easy as sending an email. Their killer feature? **Zero markups on exchange rates.**

### Why Choose Wise?
*   **Mid-Market Exchange Rates**: You get the real rate you see on Google. No hidden 'spreads.'
*   **Simplicity & Speed**: Manage 50+ currencies from a single app. Transfers to platforms like Alipay or local banks often take minutes.
*   **Low Maintenance**: No monthly fees for active or inactive accounts, making it perfect for smaller-scale entrepreneurs.

## Why Buying a 'Fully Verified' Account is Essential

Both Payoneer and Wise employ world-class anti-fraud systems. Common registration 'landmines' include:
1.  **Account Association**: If your previous account was restricted, the system will use 'device fingerprints' to ban your new account instantly.
2.  **The POA Loop**: Wise is extremely picky about Proof of Address. Most sellers cannot provide a utility bill from a premium jurisdiction (UK/EU/US) that matches their ID.
3.  **Activation Funding**: Wise requires an initial deposit from a bank account in your name—a classic 'chicken and egg' problem for new international sellers.
