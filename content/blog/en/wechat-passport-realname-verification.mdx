## Step-by-Step WeChat Passport Verification

### Phase 1: Locating the Verification Portal
Open WeChat -> Tap 'Me' -> Tap 'Services' -> Tap 'Wallet' -> Tap 'Identity Information'.

### Phase 2: Data Entry (The Most Critical Step)
*   **Document Type**: Select 'Passport'.
*   **Name**: This is the #1 failure point. Look at the `<<<<<` symbols at the bottom of your passport. If your name is shown as `SMITH<<ALEXANDER<JAMES`, enter `SMITH ALEXANDER JAMES` exactly in that order. Do not try to swap first and last names.
*   **Passport Number**: Double-check for accuracy, ensuring letters and numbers are distinct.
*   **Validity Date**: Must be 100% accurate. If your passport is within 30 days of expiring, the system will restrict your payment functions.

### Phase 3: Document Upload
When taking the photo of your passport:
1.  **Eliminate Glare**: Reflections on the glossy laminate will cause the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to fail.
2.  **Full Frame**: Ensure all four edges of the passport page are within the viewfinder.
3.  **MRZ Clarity**: The bottom two lines of text must be perfectly sharp and readable.

### Phase 4: Facial Recognition (Liveness Check)
You will be asked to perform random actions (blink, move your head). **Pro Tip:** Avoid standing directly under a bright light or in front of a window, as this creates shadows that prevent the AI from matching your face to your passport photo.

### Phase 5: International Card Binding (Strongly Recommended)
While the passport alone can verify your identity, linking a Visa or Mastercard will push your success rate to over 95%. WeChat uses the bank’s data to further confirm your identity in real-time.

### Q1: Do I need a Chinese bank card for verification?
**Ans:** No. As of 2026, a passport + an international credit card is enough. However, to withdraw funds from your WeChat balance, a mainland Chinese bank account is still required.

### Q2: Can I change my identity information later?
**Ans:** You cannot change the name once verified. If you get a new passport, you can update the 'Validity Date' and 'Passport Number' within the Identity Information section.

**Ans:** WeChat uses bank-grade encryption. Real-name verification is a mandatory compliance requirement under the People's Bank of China (PBOC) regulations for non-bank payment institutions.
