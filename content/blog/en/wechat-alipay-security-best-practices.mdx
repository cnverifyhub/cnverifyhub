# WeChat & Alipay Security: 2026 Survival Guide

Maintaining stability for Chinese digital accounts from abroad is an art. In 2026, the AI-driven risk control systems of Tencent and Ant Group are more sophisticated than ever. Here is how you can stay under the radar.

## The Top 3 Ban Triggers

1. **IP Teleportation**: Logging in from New York then London within minutes. This is a red flag for account hijacking.
2. **Device Blacklisting**: Using a smartphone or emulator that has a history of banned accounts.
3. **Velocity Attacks**: Adding too many friends or sending too many payments immediately after login.

## How to "Warm Up" Your Account

1. **Residential IPs Only**: Avoid datacenter VPNs. Use high-quality residential proxies that match your intended location.
2. **Slow Engagement**: Like a few posts, browse the "Discover" tab, and make a small donation to a charity within the app to build trust.
3. **Bind a Global Number**: Link your own international phone number for 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication).
