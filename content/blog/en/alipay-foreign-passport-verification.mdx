## TL;DR: Secrets to a Successful Alipay Verification

*   **Lighting is King**: Perform facial recognition and passport scanning in bright, natural light. Avoid glare on the plastic laminate.
*   **The MRZ Rule**: Always enter your name exactly as it appears in the Machine-Readable Zone (the two lines of text at the bottom of your passport photo page).
*   **Card Supplement**: Linking an international Visa or Mastercard can significantly boost your verification success rate.
*   **Human Support**: If automated scans fail three times, switch to 'Manual Review' and provide a photo of yourself holding your passport.

## Step-by-Step Alipay Passport Verification

### Phase 1: Preparation
Ensure you have the following ready:
*   **Your Physical Passport** (Original, not a scan or copy).
*   **Latest Alipay App version** (International or standard version).
*   **A stable internet connection** (avoid public Wi-Fi during KYC).

### Phase 2: Navigation
Open Alipay -> Tap 'Me' -> Tap 'Settings' (top-right gear icon) -> Tap 'Account & Security' -> Tap 'Identity Information'.

### Phase 3: Data Entry (The #1 Error Point)
This is where 70% of applications fail. Follow these rules strictly:
*   **Name Format**: Use the format from the MRZ at the bottom of your passport. If your passport says `DOE<<JOHN<MICHAEL`, enter `DOE JOHN MICHAEL` exactly like that.
*   **Passport Number**: Double-check zeros (`0`) vs. the letter `O`.
*   **Country/Region**: Must match the 'Issuing State' on your passport.

### Phase 4: Document Scanning
Use the in-app camera. **Pro Tip:** Place your passport on a dark, non-reflective surface. Hold your phone directly above it and ensure all four corners of the page are visible and the text is sharp.

### Phase 5: Facial Recognition (Liveness Check)
You will be asked to blink, open your mouth, or turn your head. Remove glasses, hats, or masks. If it fails, try standing against a plain white wall.

## Common Failure Reasons & Solutions

| Issue | Probable Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Name Mismatch** | Incorrect order of First/Last names | Check the MRZ zone at the bottom; use that exact sequence. |
| **Blurry Photo** | Camera focus or low light | Re-shoot during the day near a window. |
| **Glare/Reflection** | Overhead lights hitting the laminate | Tilt the passport 5-10 degrees to avoid direct reflections. |
| **Facial Match Failed** | Background is too busy | Stand against a solid, light-colored background. |

## FAQ: Alipay Verification for International Users

### Q1: Can I verify without a Chinese bank card?
**Ans:** Yes. In 2026, Alipay supports verification using only your passport + an international credit card (Visa/Mastercard). However, to hold large balances, a Chinese Bank Card is still recommended.

### Q2: My passport is expiring soon. Can I still verify?
**Ans:** Your passport must be valid. If it has less than 6 months of validity, the system may reject the KYC. It is better to renew your passport before starting the process.

### Q3: How many times can I try if I fail?
**Ans:** There is a daily limit. If you fail 3 times, wait 24 hours. Excessive failures with incorrect data can lead to a temporary account freeze. If stuck, use the 'Contact Support' button to upload a 'Selfie with Passport' manually.
